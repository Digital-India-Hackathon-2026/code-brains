## Idea
Problem Statement : Autonomous Agentic Engine for Real-Time Fraud Triage and Mule Account Eradication
The challenge is to develop an independent Agentic Engine (AE) to triage frauds and eradicate mule accounts in real time.
As financial transactions have become faster and more digital, so have financial crimes, such as the growing number of mule accounts to launder money and route fraud through. Legacy rule-based fraud detection systems compare transactions to static sets of rules that result in too many false positives. This leads to false declines and denies valid transactions to customers and results in instant revenue loss, and is trivially circumvented by a sophisticated fraudster. Moreover, once an anomaly is identified, the next step in triage is to a great extent manual and the illicit funds can be quickly transferred across jurisdictions. Recently, the Reserve Bank of India has introduced a system called MuleHunter.AI showed the importance of leveraging behavioural analysis for the detection of incidents, which have a critical national importance.
